One-sentence: The oversized Funnel Display statistic used in the context ("37%", "1 de 5") and organizer ("+15 años") sections.

```jsx
<Stat value="37%" label="de empresas chilenas usa IA de forma sistemática" />
<Stat value="+50" label="empresas acompañadas" accent="teal" />
```

`value` renders in Funnel Display Medium at a fluid clamp; `label` is muted body copy capped at ~22ch. `accent` colors the number: `pink` | `teal` | `blue` | `white`.
