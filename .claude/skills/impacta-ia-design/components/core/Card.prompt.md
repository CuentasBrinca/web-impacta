One-sentence: The dark raised panel behind every card on the site — hairline border, optional hover lift, optional colored top bar.

```jsx
<Card hover accent="pink" padding="var(--space-6)">…</Card>
```

`hover` adds a −4px lift and border brighten; `accent` (`pink`|`teal`|`blue`) draws a 3px top bar (used to color-code the three ejes); `padding` overrides the default. Radius `--radius-lg`, surface `--surface-card`.
