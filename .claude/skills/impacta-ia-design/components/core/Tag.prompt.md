One-sentence: Small pill chip for track labels, country tags, and metadata, tinted with any of the three eje accents.

```jsx
<Tag tone="pink">Innovación</Tag>
<Tag tone="teal" solid>Escalamiento</Tag>
<Tag tone="neutral">Chile</Tag>
```

Tones: `neutral` | `pink` | `teal` | `blue`. `solid` fills with the accent color and white text instead of the tinted-outline default. Body font, full pill radius.
