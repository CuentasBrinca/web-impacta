One-sentence: Custom magenta checkbox with a rich (link-bearing) label for consent lines and toggles.

```jsx
<Checkbox defaultChecked>
  Acepto que Brinca trate mis datos conforme a la <a href="#">política de privacidad</a>.
</Checkbox>
```

Native checkbox visually replaced: rounded box, white check SVG, fills magenta when on. Label wraps as body copy; children may include links.
